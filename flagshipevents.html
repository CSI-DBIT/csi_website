Mumbai Hackathon

Mumbai Hackathon is Annual Open Source Hackathon organized by FOSS United and Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT). Every year, we welcome students, developers and designers from across the country to create incredible open source projects at Mumbai's largest Open Source Hackathon.
